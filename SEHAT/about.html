ABOUT US

SEHAT is an initiative towards bridging the gap between the government and the people in this pandemic. We strive to make every citizen to get acknowledged with the actions and norms which the govenment has been indulging inorder to prevent the spread of virus. The main motive of our actions is to spread the awareness about the current situation , its adverse effects on human society and also unblind the sufficent measures which must be taken by every citizen that are being proposed by the govenment .
SEHAT updates accordingly to the current norms and actions that are taken by the government and provide it to every one in much simpler and condensed manner so that its readable , understandable to everyone.ALL the information that is being presented in SEHAT is taken directly from the government and no false data or inappropriate data will be shared so.
